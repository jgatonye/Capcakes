HOW TO MAKE CUPCAKES

Kindly follow the following simple procdures to make sweet cupcakes

Ingrediates

  1. One and half cups all-purpose flour.
  2. 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder.
  3. 1/4 teaspoon fine salt
  4. 2 large eggs, at room temperature.
  5. 2/3 cap sugar.
  6. 1 1/2 sticks unsalted batter, melted
  7. Two tea spoons pure vanilla extract.
  8. 1/2 cap milk
